What is this person remembered for? Maya Angelou is most remembered as being a respected spokesperson for African Americans and women. She wrote poems, books, music from a different point of view and many people really like that. She had a big impact on the civil rights movement when she helped raise money, organize events and help Martin Luther King Jr. and Malcolm X. Maya Angelou was a mentor and close friend to Oprah Winfrey. She was called the People’s poet because she wrote for the people. One of the note-worthy events in her life was in 1993, Angelou recited her poem "On the Pulse of Morning" at the inauguration of President Bill Clinton. She also received the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2011 from President Barack Obama
